Duck-Billed Platypus

Duck-billed platypuses are small, shy animals with a flattened head and body to help them glide through the water.

  • Type: Mammal
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 15 Inches Long
  • Weight: 3 Pounds

The duck-billed platypus's head and body grow to about 15 inches and its tail grows to about 5 inches long. They have an amazing snout that looks like a duck's bill, but is actually quite soft and covered with thousands of receptors that help the platypus detect prey. Platypuses spend most of their time alone, sleeping or eating.
